Kid Classes Pre-Ballet, Ballet, Preschool, Kids Combo

Kids classes start at 18 months and go until 12 years old. We have class options everyday except Sunday. Kids classes start at $45 a month.

Youth Classes Pom, Pointe, Jazz, Lyrical/Contemporary, Hip Hop, Performace

Our Youth Classes are 12 years old to 18 years old. These classes start at $45 a month with discounts if dancer is taking more than one class. No experience needed for these classes.

Adult Classes Improv, Hip Hop, Barre Fitness

Adult classes are for anyone 18 years or older. We have a range of dance experience classes from "I've never danced before" to "Its been years" to experienced dancers. Classes start at $65 a month.
